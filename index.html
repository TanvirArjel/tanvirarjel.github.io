About Me

It’s me, Tanvir Ahmad Arjel, hailed from Mohanganj, Netrakona. I completed my S.S.C from Mohanganj Pilot Govt. High School, Mohanganj and H.S.C from Notre Dame College, Dhaka. However, I am a little bit late starter in the field of programming. Now i am working in a famous software company in Bangladesh as a Junior Software Engineer. Programming, I think, is not my profession but passion. Alhamdulillah I never get bored or tired of programming. I can code all day long. Besides programming, I like to read books and do mobile photography in leisure time.
